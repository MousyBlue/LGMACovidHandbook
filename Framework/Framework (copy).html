Framework Matrix

The structure of the framework is simply based on the following tiered progression;

  1. Broad matrix identifying response and recovery elements, ie things that can be influenced/controlled.
  2. Derivation of the internal (organisation business continuity) and external (community disaster management) objectives for each of the elements within the matrix.
  3. Links to second tier response matrices for each of the derived objectives setting out potential actions for each.
  4. Links to third tier template project plans for each action and download them for your use.
  5. Links to other third tier template tools (eg risk assessment pro-forma) and download them for your use.
  6. Links to useful references.

Just click on each of the cells in the table below to follow the respective links and then access templates and references by clicking on the links in the second tier matrices.
