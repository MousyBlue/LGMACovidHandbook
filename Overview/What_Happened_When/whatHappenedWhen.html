What Happened When

Find out more about the chronological sequence of events by clicking the icons shown on the timeline to the right.

30 Jan 2020

The WHO Director General declared the novel coronavirus outbreak a public health emergency of international concern (PHEIC), WHO's highest level of alarm.


15 Mar 2020

First meeting of National Cabinet where leaders banned cruise ships, non-essential public gatherings of more than 500 people were outlawed, and an edict for all international arrivals to self-isolate for 14 days was agreed to.


24 Mar 2020

The Premier announced that Queensland would restrict access at the border followed by the first death of a COVID patient the next day.


30 Mar 2020

Queensland entered its first lockdown, with travel outside the home banned except for essential reasons.
